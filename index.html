The 4-Phase Strategic Plan

An automated, repeatable process to transform our pipeline and empower our Account Executives.

Phase 1: Data Foundation

Cleanse CAPP data, standardize account hierarchies, and build a custom "TAM Prospect" data structure in Salesforce to house the complete 10-year market model.

Phase 2: AI-Powered Gap Analysis

Deploy an AI-driven job in Salesforce to automatically compare the TAM against the active pipeline, identifying and scoring every single gap.

Phase 3: AE Workflow Integration

Create a "frictionless" AE experience with automated tasking, a dedicated gap closure dashboard, and a "one-click" process to convert TAM prospects into qualified opportunities.

Phase 4: Performance Tracking

Build leadership dashboards in Tableau to track new KPIs, and establish a formal feedback loop for AEs to continuously refine and improve the TAM model over time.
